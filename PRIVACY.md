# Privacy Policy for WhatDayIsIt

**Last updated:** December 2024

## Overview

WhatDayIsIt is a browser extension that highlights dates on web pages and shows what day of the week they fall on. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.**

Specifically, WhatDayIsIt:

- ❌ Does NOT collect personal information
- ❌ Does NOT track your browsing history
- ❌ Does NOT send any data to external servers
- ❌ Does NOT use analytics or tracking
- ❌ Does NOT store any data outside your browser

## Data Storage

The extension stores only your preferences (enabled/disabled state, highlight settings, icon position) locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in `chrome.storage.sync` API. This data:

- Stays on your device and synced Chrome browsers
- Is never transmitted to us or any third party
- Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension

## Permissions

The extension requires these permissions:

- **activeTab**: To highlight dates on the current page you're viewing
- **storage**: To save your preferences locally

## Third Parties

We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any information.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

---

**Summary: WhatDayIsIt runs entirely in your browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Ever.**
